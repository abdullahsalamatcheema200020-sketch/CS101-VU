Artificial Intelligence (AI) = building machines that can do tasks which normally need human intelligence. 🤖 Examples around you: • ChatGPT — answers any question 💬 • Google Maps — finds best route 🗺️ • Face unlock on phones 📱 • Netflix recommendations 🎬 • Voice assistants (Siri, Alexa) 🎤 The Turing Test Proposed by Alan Turing in 1950 to test if a machine is "intelligent": 🧪 The test: A human chats with something (unseen) — could be a computer or another human. If the human CAN'T tell which is the computer → the AI passes! ✅ Modern AI like ChatGPT often passes this test! 🎉 Main Areas of AI Computer Vision Machines that "see"! Image analysis uses: Edge enhancement — find shapes Smoothing — reduce noise Pattern recognition NLP Natural Language Processing Syntactic — grammar Semantic — meaning Contextual — situation Machine Learning Machines learn from data — get better with experience! 📈 Neural Networks Inspired by the human brain — millions of connected neurons! Intelligent Agents An intelligent agent is a program that: 👀 Perceives its environment (sensors) 🧠 Thinks about what to do 🤖 Acts on the environment (actuators) 🚗 Example: Self-driving car Sees road (cameras) → Thinks about traffic → Turns wheel/brakes! The Future of AI AI is changing every field: 🏥 Healthcare Diagnosing diseases from X-rays 🚗 Self-Driving Cars Tesla, Waymo 🎨 Creativity Art, music, writing 🤖 Robots Factories, surgery, space 🎓 As a CS student, YOU will shape this future!
